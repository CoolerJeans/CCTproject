The ClueFinders 3rd Grade Adventures: The Mystery of Mathra

Introduction:

The ClueFinders 3rd Grade Adventures: The Mystery of Mathra was released in 1998 by the Learning Company. Completed with an in depth plot, the player is a young kid who investigates a case about disappearing animals with three friends. During the game, the player must use logic and critical thinking to uncover the mystery. This mystery and adventure game is graphics driven.

Educational Value

In this game, the player travels through four different geographical regions. Each geographical region tests the player on a different subject between math, logic, geography and science/language arts. As the name suggests, this game is aimed towards younger students, in grade three.

The Legacy

Despite being less popular than previous games, The ClueFinders 3rd Grade Adventures: The Mystery of Mathra was ranked first place in an online pole for “The Best Educational Games”.
