Introduction:

Where in the World is Carmen Sandiego? was originally released in 1985 by Broderbund Software. The player is a detective who must use their global geographical knowledge to catch the thief Carmen Sandiego and her goons. To do this, they must decipher and use clues they pick up along the way. Despite having improved graphics in comparison to the aforementioned games, Where in the World is Carmen Sandiego? is a text based game. The core mechanics of the game include reading clues, solving clues, and catching flights.

Educational Value

The education goal of this game is to teach the player about geography and culture. More than knowing names of countries and their capitals, clues can be in the form of landmarks and currency. As well, the player can learn about the cultural significance of the item stolen (whether it be a painting or an artifact).

The Legacy

More than being a game, the transmedia of this game extends to sequels, TV shows and an interactive Netflix series (Classic Reload, 2025). Many spinoff games have been created after the original, with more specific learning goals such as “Where in the U.S.A is Carmen Sandiego” (1996) and “Carmen Sandiego: Adventures in Math” (2010).
