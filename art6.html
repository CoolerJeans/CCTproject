Introduction:

Super Solvers: Treasure Mountain! was published in 1990 by The Learning Company. This game is graphics based, with considerably better visuals and less text than the previous artifacts in this exhibit. In this game, treasures are hidden all throughout the mountain for the player to find. Players must catch elves and answer their math questions to get hints on where the treasures can be found. Overall, this puzzle game focuses on problem solving skills, exploration, foresight strategy, and clue searching. The core mechanics of the game include moving the character, catching elves, answering their questions, uncovering riddles, and finding treasures.

Educational Value

This game is targeted towards kids from kindergarten to grade four. The main learning objective revolves around problem solving, mathematics, and reading comprehension.

The Legacy

This adventure game was able to create fond memories for many, being “a nostalgic favorite for many” (Classic Reload, 2025). Based on its success, its “ influence can be seen in later educational titles” (Classic Reload, 2025).
